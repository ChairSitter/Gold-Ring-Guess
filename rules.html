BACK TO GOLD RING GUESS:

Rules

    Object: Get the lowest total in 10 rounds.

    Score Medal
    100 or less BRONZE
    50 or less SILVER
    0 or less GOLD

    Click PLAY to start a round.

    Each round, the circles are randomly filled white, light gray, dark gray, or black.

    The grid has a total value based on adding 3 values: Colors score, In-a-Row score, and Gold Ring score.
    Your job is to guess the total value as close as possible in 10 seconds.
    The difference is calculated each round, and a Bonus is subtracted based on dividing the value of the grid by 10, because high value grids are harder to guess.
    An exact guess will multiply the bonus by 2. Aim for the lowest score in 10 rounds!


    Colors score- Add up all circles by color value:
    White = 1pt
    LGray = 2 pts
    D Gray = 3 pts
    Black = 4 pts


    In-a-Row score- 3, 4, or 5 of the same color horizontal, vertical, or diagonal score as follows:
    3-in-a-row = 9 x color value
    4-in-a-row = 16 x color value
    5-in-a-row = 25 x color value


    Gold Ring score- each gold ring gives a bonus:
    10 x color value
